1. Introduction

Kōhaku Studio ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you visit our website, place an order, or contact us.

By using our site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identification data: full name, email address, shipping address, phone number.
  • Payment data: billing address and payment details are processed by our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Order data: items purchased, order date, order value, delivery preferences.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring URL, time spent on pages.
  • Communications: any messages you send to us via email or contact forms.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and customer support.
  • Communicate about your order, respond to inquiries, and provide after-sales support.
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates, return notices).
  • Send marketing communications, only when you have opted in.
  • Improve our website, products, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including tax and consumer protection laws.
  • Prevent fraud and protect the security of our platform.

4. Sharing with third parties

We share personal data only with service providers who help us operate our business, and only to the extent necessary. These may include:

  • Payment processors (e.g., our checkout partner) for handling transactions.
  • Shipping and logistics carriers to deliver your order.
  • Email service providers for order and marketing communications.
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel) for website performance measurement.
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and personalize your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some features.

We use both first-party and third-party cookies. Third-party cookies may include those from Google, Meta, and our analytics providers.

6. Data retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Order records are typically retained for the period required by applicable tax and commercial laws.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International transfers

Because our team collaborates between Brazil and Japan, and because some of our service providers are located outside your country of residence, your personal data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. When we transfer data, we take steps to ensure it receives an adequate level of protection.

10. Children's privacy

Our website and products are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
