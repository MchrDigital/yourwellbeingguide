Before you write

Frequently asked

How long does shipping take?

Orders are prepared within 3 business days and ship worldwide. Delivery typically takes 5–14 business days depending on destination.

Can I return a piece?

Yes. Unused pieces can be returned within 14 days of delivery. Contact us with your order number to start the process.

Are variations normal?

Each piece is handmade, so small variations in color, texture, and finish are part of the craft — never a defect.

Do you take custom commissions?

Yes. For custom pieces, tea sets, or corporate gifting, select "Custom commission" in the form above and share your idea.
